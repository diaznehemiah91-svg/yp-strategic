## Sponsorship Opportunities (Recommended for Your Niche)

### 1. Direct Sponsorships - Finance & Defense Tech Companies
Target these types of sponsors:
- Defense contractors (Lockheed Martin, RTX, Northrop Grumman, etc.)
- Cybersecurity firms (CrowdStrike, Mandiant, Fortinet, etc.)
- Crypto/fintech platforms (Coinbase, Kraken, FTX-like platforms)
- Investment firms and trading platforms
- Intelligence/research firms

**Approach:**
- Create a "Sponsorship" or "Partners" page
- Email list of potential sponsors with media kit
- Offer brand mentions, sponsored articles, or dedicated sections
- Typical rates: $500-$5000+ per month depending on traffic
